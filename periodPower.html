Period Power

Description

The hormone cycle of people who menstruate lasts 28 days, which is in contrast to the 24-hour schedule that our society is built on. This google agent informs people who menstruate how they can optimize their lives to be in accorrdance with their natural hormone cycle. It can answer questions about nutrition and exercise, and might one day include more categories such as planning your social calendar or planning when to take on your most difficult tasks. It also provides insight on how you can relieve any symptoms associated with your menstruation cycle.

Tech Specs

This was built using Google's Dialogflow.
